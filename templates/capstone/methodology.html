Methodology of Calculation

Life Quality Score

Life Quality Score (higher is better) is an estimated measurement of the overall life quality in a specific city. In the calculation of the score, we take into account Cost of Living to Income Ratio (lower is better), Housing Expense to Income Ratio (lower is better), Health Care Index (higher is better), Crime Index (higher is better), Safety Index (higher is better), and Food Option Index (higher is better).

The current formula:

Sum of scores from user selected options / Number of selected options

Sum of scores from users options * = (100 - cost of living to Income ration) + (100 - housing expense to income radio) + healthcare index + crime index + safety index + food option

*Note: only include user selected options
Cost of Living to Income Ratio

The Cost of Living to Income Ratio reflects the percentage of living expense in a user's total monthly income.

Cost of Living to Income Ratio = Total expense* / monthly income

*Note: Total expense including cost of living expense and/or child care expense(per user's selection)
Housing Expense to Income Ratio

The Housing Expense (lower is better) to Income Radio reflects the percentage of housing expense in a user's total monthly income.

Housing Expense to Income Ratio = Housing expense* / monthly income

*Note: Monthly rent expense or monthly mortgage payment
Health Care Index

Health Care Index (higher is better) is an estimation of the overall health care and medical resources for a specific city.

This index along with other data in the health care report are cited from survey results in Numbeo.com

Crime Index

Crime Index is an estimation of overall crime level in a specific city.

This index along with other data in the crime report are cited from survey results in Numbeo.com

Safety Index

Safety Index is an estimation of overall safety level in a specific city.

This index along with other data in the safety report are cited from survey results in Numbeo.com

Food Option Index

Food Option Index reflects the culinary diversity ranking in a specific city.

This index along with other data in the food option report are cited from Trulia.com
